Overview

The Smart Vehicle Health Monitoring System Using OBD - II is an intelligent platform designed to predict vehicle health issues before they lead to critical failures. By Levaraging advanced Long Short Term Memory (LSTM) neural networks, this system continously analyzes historical vehicle data obtained by OBD - II (On-Board Diagnostic) Dataset from Kaggle.
Unlike traditional tools that only detects faults after they occur, SVHM focuses on predictive maintenance, Identifying early signs of degradation in vehicle performance.

What should you do?

  • Step 1: Select DataSet: Choose one of the three available datasets provided on the platform.
  • Step 2: Choose Analysis Mode: LSTM mode or Data Visualization Mode
  • Step 3: Results: For LSTM Model: It will show health score, error analysis graphs, anomalies detection and health status. For Data Visualization: It will help you create interactive graphs, illustrate trends and correlation.
